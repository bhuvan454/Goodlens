Goodlens - A Neighborhood Watch

Goodlens is a neighborhood watch program developing for kansas city communities under National Science Foundation program. In this program, an extensive understanding of a given locality was aimed with help of state of the art deep learning and Satilite Imaging. Primary objective is to study the abandment of houses in any area. With data from various sources, an quantitative approch to understand a locality is explored.
